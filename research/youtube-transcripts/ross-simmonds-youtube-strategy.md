## Notes / transcript summary

Key ideas captured from Ross Simmonds’ long-form content:

- YouTube is underutilized by B2B SaaS brands compared with its search and discovery potential.
- Video content can rank for high-intent queries such as demos, comparisons, reviews, and category searches.
- Strong YouTube strategy for B2B SaaS is not just about production quality; it is about matching search intent and solving buyer questions.
- Existing video libraries can often be re-optimized through titles, descriptions, thumbnails, and keyword targeting.
- Video content can support broader organic growth by feeding SEO, repurposing, and multi-channel distribution.
- B2B teams should think of YouTube not only as a brand channel but also as a demand capture and discovery channel.
